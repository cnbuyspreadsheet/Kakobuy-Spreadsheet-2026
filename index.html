Frequently Asked Questions

What is the Kakobuy Spreadsheet?

It’s a curated list of the best finds you can order through Kakobuy, organized by the AstroReps community. The spreadsheet links directly to AstroReps’ category pages for easy browsing.

How often is the spreadsheet updated?

New finds are added weekly to reflect the latest drops – you’ll always have fresh Kakobuy 2026 spreadsheet content.

Can I get QC photos for every item?

Yes! Every AstroReps category page shows Kakobuy QC photos for each listed product, so you can inspect quality before you buy.

Which categories are most popular?

The Kakobuy shoes spreadsheet and Kakobuy hoodie spreadsheet pages are fan favorites, but the full range covers everything from hats to underwear.

How do I order from Taobao, Weidian, or 1688 using Kakobuy?

Simply copy the item link from the spreadsheet (or AstroReps) and paste it into your Kakobuy order form. The agent handles the rest – including QC photos and shipping.
